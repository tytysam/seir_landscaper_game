Landscape Tycoon

How to Play

Spend your days becoming a landscaping tycoon!

Win the game by having more than $1,000 at once!

    Commands:
  • 'stop' to quit the game
  • 'landscape' to cut the grass
  • 'buy' to purchase the tool

    Tools:
  • A pair of Rusty Scissors — $5
  • An Old-timey Push-Mower – $50
  • A fancy battery-powered lawnmower – $250
  • A team of college students – $500

TIP: Different tools will help you landscape faster, but you can only upgrade to the next tool when you have enough money.
