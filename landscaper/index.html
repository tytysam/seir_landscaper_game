Landscape Tycoon

How to Play

Spend your days becoming a landscaping tycoon!

Different tools will help you landscape faster, but you can only upgrade to the next tool when you have enough money.

Win the game by having more than $1,000 at once!

Tools:

  • A pair of Rusty Scissors — $5
  • An Old-timey Push-Mower – $50
  • A fancy battery-powered lawnmower – $250
  • A team of college students – $500
